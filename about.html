Be who you are

   I was born in Vietnam on Feb 2, 1990 at a remote poor village surronding with mountain and forest, named Phuoc Cat I. My childhood is associated with many beautiful memories, with rivers, mountains, and classrooms built of wood.

   I loved reading adventurous books, cooking, and spending time with friends. I and my friends have played from little kid to adulthood, and we have promised to keep that companionship forever.

   After two years of studying at a school in my hometown, I asked my parents' permission to enroll in a prestigious school to be able to enter university, Nguyen Khuyen High School. Luckily, I passed the university exams with very high scores, graduated, and matriculated at the University of Mediacation Ho Chi Minh.
